# Privacy Policy

**Padel Counter** is a free, open-source Android application.

## Data Collection

This app does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

- No user accounts or login required
- No analytics or tracking
- No internet connection required or used
- No data is sent to any server
- No cookies or local storage of personal information
- Bluetooth is used only for receiving button presses from paired HID devices — no data is transmitted

## Permissions

The app requires no special permissions. Bluetooth HID devices work through standard Android system pairing without any app-level Bluetooth permissions.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect data.

## Changes

If this policy changes, updates will be posted in this file in the app's source repository.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/dominiklindorfer/Padelcounter).
